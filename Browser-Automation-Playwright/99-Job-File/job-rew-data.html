Minimum Qualifications:

Responsibilities & Context

About the company:

  • Genuity Systems Ltd. is a Dhaka based customised software, IP related applications development, and IT services company with global outlook. The Company was established in the year 2003 in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It has by now established itself as one of the prestigious entities providing IT and telecommunications related services not only in Bangladesh but also in overseas countries.

  • Genuity provided services include but are not limited to Contact Center solutions, IVR solutions, AI Voice/Chat Bot solutions, Mobile Banking apps, Mobile applications, Smart Business Phone solutions and advanced IT training.

About the Job Post:

  • We are seeking an innovative “Software Engineer (Machine Learning)” to work as software developers and software engineers to ensure the development of our running projects.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, test, deploy, maintain and improve high performance business applications

  • Solve issues on current applications

  • Work closely with development team to ensure accurate integration of Machine Learning Models to live platform

  • Analyse data to scale current models

  • Communicate with team members to identify new data insight

  • Manage individual project priorities, deadlines and deliverables
